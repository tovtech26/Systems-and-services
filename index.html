About the firm

Experienced engineering leadership for complex client environments.

Systems & Services Engineers (Pty) Ltd is one of Botswana's experienced engineering consultancy and project management companies, serving commercial developments, office blocks, factories, laboratories, auditoriums, health facilities, military installations, mining work, water and sanitation infrastructure, SCADA, telemetry, and associated building services.

The firm operates from approximately 430m2 of fully equipped office space in Gaborone International Finance Park, supported by high-speed communication infrastructure and a team with international experience across London, New York and Africa.
